About Us

Created in 1997 as a computer and electronics repairs shop at Molyko-Buea, Goodmann has crawled over the years to embrace other emerging aspects of the IT Industry, and is slowly routing towards the top. “You cannot build a reputation on what you are going to do-- Henry Ford” This explains why we aren’t at the top yet.


Goodmann dwells in the domain of Information and communication technology providing both services and training. Goodmann Enterprise is a business entity providing un-parallel quality IT services, while the Goodman Institute of Business and Computing (GIBC) is a registered vocational training institute (reg no. 354/MINEFOP/SG/DFOP/SDGSF/SACD of 14th November 2016) taking care of training and testing.


As a training entity, its mission is to empower professionals through globally recognized certifications. In order to pursue this mission GIBC keeps seeking partnerships with leading institutions in the world and in this quest has partnered with Cisco Systems as an accredited Cisco Networking Academy (academy ID 400055908), providing a wide range of IT certification courses with globally recognized Certifications. Goodmann is also a Pearson Vue Authorized Testing Center (site 57081) providing a facility for computer based testing, where anyone who seeks to obtain a globally recognized certification in any domain of study can be tested and the corresponding certification awarded by the corresponding certification body.
